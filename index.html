Workshop: Analysis of PDEs in Dalian

May 25-29, 2026

Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China

The Workshop “Analysis of PDEs in Dalian,” hosted by Dalian University of Technology, will bring together leading experts and promising young researchers in the analysis of partial differential equations, with particular focus on fluid mechanics, water-wave problems, kinetic theory, and Hamiltonian PDEs. The workshop aims to foster academic exchange and collaboration between early-career researchers and established scholars, providing a dedicated platform to discuss the latest advances, emerging challenges, and innovative analytical techniques in PDE theory.

Registration

Registration for the workshop is free but required. To complete your registration, please send the following documents to Mr. Jiashun Liu at (liujiashun(at)mail.dlut.edu.cn).
  • CV
  • motivation letter
  • recommendation letter to be sent directly from the supervisor/mentor
The deadline for registration is March 15th, 2026. All the local expenses will be covered by Dalian University of Technology, including accommodation and meals. If you register successfully, a confirmation email will be sent to you.


Participants who are interested in applying for further financial support are recommended to present posters in the poster session; and please kindly inform us (liujiashun(at)mail.dlut.edu.cn).
  • title and abstract of the proposed presentation
  • requested amount of financial support
