Workshop: Analysis of PDEs in Dalian

May 25-29, 2026

Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China

The Workshop "Analysis of PDEs in Dalian", hosted by Dalian University of Technology, will bring together leading experts and young researchers in the analysis of partial differential equations, with particular focus on fluid mechanics, water-wave problems, kinetic theory, and Hamiltonian PDEs. The workshop aims to foster academic exchange and collaboration between early-career researchers and established scholars, providing a dedicated platform to discuss the latest advances, emerging challenges, and innovative analytical techniques in PDE theory.

  • CV
  • motivation letter
  • expected arrival and departure times
  • recommendation letter to be sent directly from the supervisor/mentor
