Workshop: Analysis of PDEs in Dalian

May 25th-29th, 2026

Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China

Introduction

The Workshop "Analysis of PDEs in Dalian", hosted by Dalian University of Technology, will bring together young researchers and leading experts in the field of analysis and partial differential equations, such as fluid mechanics, Hamiltonian PDEs and computer-assisted proofs. We aim at promoting academic exchanges and collaborations among early-career scientists and experts, offering a dedicated platform to discuss recent advances, emerging challenges, and innovative analysis methods in PDE theory.
