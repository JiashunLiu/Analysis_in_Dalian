Workshop: Analysis of PDEs in Dalian

May 25th-29th, 2026

Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China

Introduction

The Workshop on Analysis of PDEs in Dalian, hosted by Dalian University of Technology, will bring together young researchers and leading experts in the field of partial differential equations. This workshop is designed to promote academic exchange and collaboration among early-career scientists, offering a dedicated platform to discuss recent advances, emerging challenges, and innovative methods in PDE analysis.
The workshop organizers will provide accommodation in Dalian for participants. Limited financial support is also available to participants who deliver a scientific talk at the workshop. Interested applicants should submit their request for support along with the title/abstract of their proposed presentation via email to liujiashun@mail.dlut.edu.cn.
